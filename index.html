ALIT Web

The public website and admin portal for the Australian Leading Institute of Technology — course catalogue, online enrolment, an LLN diagnostic assessment system, and a staff dashboard.

RTO 45156  ·  CRICOS 03981M  ·  Melbourne, VIC

Overview

ALIT Web is a single application serving both prospective students and internal staff. It is built on Next.js and backed by Supabase for data and authentication, deployed on Vercel.

Public website
Course catalogue, newsroom, prospectus, and contact information.
Online enrolment
A multi-step application form for domestic and international students.
LLN assessment
A diagnostic Language, Literacy & Numeracy tool used to tailor learner support.
Admin dashboard
Staff portal for managing applications, courses, users, and LLN results.

Architecture

The frontend runs on Vercel and talks to Supabase for all data and authentication. Public pages, the enrolment flow, the LLN assessment, and the admin dashboard all share the same Supabase backend.

LLN system

The Language, Literacy & Numeracy assessment is a diagnostic tool, not a pass/fail test. A learner completes a 38-question assessment across five domains, and the results produce a profile that helps staff tailor support.

flowchart LR
    W[Welcome] --> S1[Study Area]
    S1 --> S2[Course]
    S2 --> C[Confirm]
    C --> D[Learner Details]
    D --> Q[Quiz - 38 Questions]
    Q --> R[Results and PDF Report]

Figure 2 — The learner journey through the LLN assessment.

Assessment domains

ReadingComprehension and interpretation of written material
WritingWritten communication and structure
Oral communicationSpoken understanding and expression
NumeracyPractical mathematical reasoning
Digital literacyUse of digital tools and devices
