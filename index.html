What it is

Public website

Course catalogue, newsroom, prospectus and contact — the public face of ALIT.

LLN assessment

A diagnostic Language, Literacy & Numeracy tool that tailors learner support.

Staff portal to manage applications, courses, users and LLN results.

Feature Highlight

The LLN Assessment System

A diagnostic Language, Literacy & Numeracy tool — not a pass/fail test. It assesses learners across five domains and generates a profile that helps staff tailor support. 

flowchart LR
    W[Welcome] --> S1[Study Area]
    S1 --> S2[Course]
    S2 --> C[Confirm]
    C --> D[Learner Details]
    D --> Q[Quiz · 38 questions]
    Q --> R[Results + PDF report]

Five domains assessed: Reading · Writing · Oral Communication · Numeracy · Digital Literacy
